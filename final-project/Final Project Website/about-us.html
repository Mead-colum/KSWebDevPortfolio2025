Our Story

A Dog's Love ADL is an organization created after our founder found his true self.

Kobe J. Stevens once a beloved dog, is now a beloved leader.
He and his owner created ADL as a way to reach out to other owners and show them the potential of their dogs

The potential of my dog?

All dogs are reincarnated primordial beings and they were made to take this form as punishment for the mistake of wiping the dinosaurs as they fought for the earth.

In the aftermath all the beings agreed they would wipe their memories and cast themselves down to live among the animals left, however the instinct to grow and evolve stayed present.

humans ‘quickly’ became the dominant species on earth so the reason dogs have become so personable to humans is that dogs knew it would eventually lead to their reevolution

So what does this mean?

Your love will nuture your dog and push them closer to their previous form. Every instance of what could be considered a "quirk" is your dog CALLING to their primordial self. Join us! and share your dog's progress with a community that wants the best for their pets.
